Rock Genres

Genre Description Notable Bands
Classic Rock An umbrella term for rock music from the late 1960s to the early 1980s. The Beatles, Led Zeppelin, The Rolling Stones
Punk Rock A fast-paced and energetic genre with rebellious themes. The Ramones, Sex Pistols, The Clash
Hard Rock A fast-paced and energetic genre with rebellious themes. AC/DC, Deep Purple, Guns N' Roses
Alternative Rock Emerged in the 1980s, characterized by a departure from mainstream and a fusion of various styles. R.E.M., Radiohead, Foo Fighters
Progressive Rock Known for its complex compositions and experimental approach. Pink Floyd, Yes, Genesis
Indie Rock Independent and often associated with DIY aesthetics. The Strokes, Arcade Fire, Arctic Monkeys
Psychedelic Rock Influenced by psychedelia, this genre often incorporates experimental sounds and unconventional song structures. The Doors, Jefferson Airplane, Pink Floyd
