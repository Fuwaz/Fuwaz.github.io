US History Flashcards

Who was the first President of the United States?
George Washington.
Which document outlined the original framework of the U.S. government?
The U.S. Constitution.
What event prompted the U.S. to enter World War II?
The attack on Pearl Harbor.
Who delivered the "I Have a Dream" speech?
Martin Luther King Jr.
Which amendment to the U.S. Constitution abolished slavery?
The 13th Amendment.
Who was the U.S. President during World War II?
Franklin D. Roosevelt.
Which U.S. President issued the Emancipation Proclamation?
Abraham Lincoln.
Which U.S. President spent the longest time in office?
Franklin D. Roosevelt.
